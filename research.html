Research

This pages states some details about the research work I am currently doing and have done.


Right now, I am working under Dr. Haiying shen in Pervasive Communications Laboratory. My work is supervised by Ankur Sarker. We are focusing on smart building designs.

I gather temperature data (temperature, humidity, etc) online and processing them. Combined with historical occupancy data, they will then be fed to the occupancy model to improve its accuracy.

Update (08/29/2019): Now we have moved on to the stage of improving the prediction accuracy of our machine learning model. We are trying different ways (criteria) of selecting training data. Selected data is then fed to the model to make predictions of parameters such as temperature and humidity in the office.
